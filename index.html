Jungle Survival Map

A co-op Ghost commando survival map about scavenging the jungle, crafting weapons, upgrading your hero, and surviving a Zerg ecosystem that evolves over time.

JS Jungle Survival

Co-op Ghost survival

Survive the jungle for five nights.

Drop in as a small squad of Ghost commandos. Scout during the day, gather what you can, build lighted footholds, and hold together when the jungle comes alive at night.

View the repo

What players should feel

You are never managing a huge army. You are controlling one fragile, upgradeable commando with a few teammates nearby. Every trip away from light should feel like a choice: risk the dark for better resources, or regroup before the next wave hits.

How the map works

The survival system

The loop, resources, enemies, and upgrades all feed into the same pressure cycle: prepare while you can, survive when the jungle pushes back, then move before your safe pocket becomes a trap.

Core loop

Scavenge, build, survive, move.

Each day and night lasts 22 minutes. Days are for scouting and preparation. Nights are for holding your nerve.

01

Day: Scout & Harvest

Explore freely, find resource pockets, gather minerals from trees, rocks, crystals, bushes, and biomass.

→
02

Craft & Upgrade

Craft one active weapon, upgrade your Ghost, share minerals, and prepare the squad for the next push.

→
03

Build a Safe Pocket

Place lights, walls, turrets, and regeneration stations. Lights brighten the area and block nearby spawns.

↓
04

Night: Survive Waves

Zerg pressure spikes. Ghost control, teamwork, weapon choice, and light placement matter most.

←
05

The Jungle Evolves

Mobs advance Black → Brown → Orange → Red. Time drives evolution; kills and exploration can accelerate it.

←
06

Deconstruct & Move

Break down structures, relocate, recover from losses, and repeat until the squad survives five nights.

Death penalty: respawn at a random position, lose most minerals and your built structures, but keep upgrades and crafted progression.

Scavenging

Resources

Everything you harvest becomes minerals, keeping the economy readable while still making the jungle feel varied.

Zerg threat ladder

Enemies

Zerg units are the base mob families. Each line evolves automatically as the run progresses. Enemy name and color tell players the threat level: Black → Brown → Orange → Red.

Commando progression

Upgrades and craftable weapons

Each player grows independently. You keep unlocks and upgrades through death, but bad fights still cost resources and structures.
