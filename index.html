Jungle Survival Map

A co-op Ghost commando survival map about scavenging the jungle, crafting weapons, upgrading your hero, and surviving a Zerg ecosystem that evolves over time.

Core experience

The gameplay loop

Survive five escalating nights as Ghost commandos: scavenge, craft, build lighted safe pockets, endure night waves, and keep moving before the jungle overwhelms you.

01

Day: Scout & Harvest

Explore freely, find resource pockets, gather minerals from trees, rocks, crystals, bushes, and biomass.

→
02

Craft & Upgrade

Craft one active weapon, upgrade your Ghost, share minerals, and prepare the squad for the next push.

→
03

Build a Safe Pocket

Place lights, walls, turrets, and regeneration stations. Lights gradually brighten the area and block nearby spawns.

↓
04

Night: Survive Waves

Zerg pressure spikes. Skillful Ghost control, teamwork, weapon choice, and light placement matter most.

←
05

The Jungle Evolves

Mobs advance Black → Brown → Orange → Red. Time drives evolution; kills and exploration can accelerate it.

←
06

Deconstruct & Move

Break down structures, relocate, recover from losses, and repeat until the squad survives five nights.

Death penalty: respawn at a random position, lose most minerals and your built structures, but keep upgrades and crafted progression.

Map pitch

What is Jungle Survival?

A quick overview of the co-op survival fantasy, player progression, and evolving Zerg threat.

Zerg threat ladder

Enemy mob variants

Zerg units are the base mob family. Each mob line upgrades automatically as the game progresses. Every evolution changes name and color so players immediately know what they are fighting: Black → Brown → Orange → Red.

Commando progression

Player upgrades and craftable weapons

Each player has their own Ghost unit DAT ID and their own weapon DAT IDs. That lets upgrades mutate one player’s stats and crafted weapons without changing anyone else.
