What you play

A survival map about small decisions under pressure.

You are not building a giant base. You are keeping one Ghost alive long enough to decide: push into the dark, hold a little longer, or tear down and move.

Night threat Black → Brown → Orange → Red

Enemy color tells the squad how far the jungle has evolved.

How the map works

The survival system

Prepare while you can, survive when the jungle pushes back, then move before your safe pocket becomes a trap.

Core loop

Scavenge, build, survive, move.

Each day and night lasts 22 minutes: scout and prepare, then hold your nerve.

Design notes

Spell, command card, and utility experiments

Scratchpad for ability/control experiments: keep the hotbar small and make power readable.

Hotbar focus

Three actives, many passive synergies

Heal, Speed, and Stun stay as the deliberate buttons; builds mostly express through weapons and passives.

Heal Self-save → ally burst → station/area pulse
Speed Escape sprint → squad reposition → reload/attack tempo window
Stun Critter catch → Maelstrom trap → elite interrupt/control field
